Everything We Do

Every format. Every memory.

From a single VHS tape to boxes of Super 8 film reels — handled in-house, never outsourced.

📼

Video Tape Digitization

VHS, Betamax, Hi8 and MiniDV transferred to digital using broadcast-quality equipment. Every tape cleaned and quality-checked.

  • VHS & VHS-C
  • S-VHS (Super VHS)
  • Betamax
  • Hi8 & Video8
  • MiniDV & Digital8
  • DVCAM & HDV
🎞️

Film Reel Digitization

Wolverine Pro Master 800 professional scanner — frame by frame, not projection. Every detail preserved.

  • 8mm Home Movies
  • Super 8mm Film
  • 16mm Film
  • Film cleaning & splicing
  • Colour restoration
  • HD & 4K output
🎵

Audio Preservation

Grandma's voice on a cassette. Dad's band on a reel-to-reel. The family record from 1962. High-fidelity digital audio.

  • Audio Cassette Tapes
  • Micro-Cassette
  • ¼" Reel-to-Reel
  • 33, 45 & 78 rpm Vinyl
  • 8-Track Audio Tapes
  • Output: MP3, WAV, FLAC
